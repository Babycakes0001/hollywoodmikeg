About Mike

A native of Oregon, Hollywood Mike G left a rising career as a Communications Behavioral Specialist focusing on autism to pursue his passion for film. Shortly after arriving in Hollywood, he landed his first major gig on NBC's The Tonight Show with Jay Leno and was quickly signed to a prominent talent agency.

Due in part to his background in Communications and Behavior, Mike was booking jobs back to back. While working on sets, he recognized a niche market demand and founded MG Casting — providing productions with talent, locations, vehicles, animals, music, clothing lines, product placement, and investors, which naturally led him into producing.

Mike is known for his work in Domino, Crank, and The Way Back, and continues to bring raw authenticity and dynamic presence to every role he takes on.
