The Maillard Reaction
Most widely practiced chemical reaction in the world. Amino acids + sugar + heat = hundreds of unique flavor compounds. Simple inputs producing complex, unpredictable outputs — that's emergence. Same principle as consciousness, jazz, and conversation.
