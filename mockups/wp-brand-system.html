01 — Marks

The two-tier logo system

Primary
The Seal

Use for: hero imagery, certificates, Soul ID watermarks, printed stationery — anywhere the mark has room to breathe.

Not for: favicons, nav bars, anything under 96px — laurels and crown filigree lose readability at small sizes.

Compact
The Key

Use for: favicon, app icon, social avatars, nav bars, loading states, embroidery, wax seals — anywhere ≤64px.

Not for: grand contexts where the full seal would fit — the compact mark is deliberately quiet.

When a document needs the heaviest possible opening — the first page of a section, the opening of a long-form piece, or the prelude to a testament — we use an engraved drop capital. The rest of the paragraph flows around it in body serif, letting the letterform carry the weight alone.
