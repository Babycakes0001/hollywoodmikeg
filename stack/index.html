Website Toolchain · Spend Plan

Three tools to build 10 websites.

A one-time $498 spend (lifetime, owned forever) plus $10/month for AI image generation. After year one, ongoing cost is $120/year.

01 · The Tools
Aceternity Pro
$199 — one-time, lifetime
What it is: A library of pre-built, professionally-designed website pieces and 15 complete website templates we can customize. Think of it like buying a fully-stocked kitchen — counters, cabinets, appliances all pre-installed — instead of building every piece from raw lumber.
What we use it for: The cinematic, luxury, futuristic-feeling sites — Worth Protecting, Sm0kBot, MyBabycakes, the dashboard. Where the website needs to feel premium and on-trend.
What we own
Pay once, ours forever. The code copies into our project — no subscription, no rental, can't be revoked.
Tailwind Plus
$299 — one-time, lifetime
What it is: The same kind of thing as Aceternity, but built for a different style — clean, professional, business-grade designs. Made by the team behind Tailwind, the most-used styling system on the web. Includes a full e-commerce template, a SaaS template, an agency template, and a "Catalyst" component kit specifically built for clean enterprise apps.
What we use it for: The four law-firm sites for Charles, the t-shirt store, any client work that needs to look serious and polished. Aceternity does cinematic; Tailwind Plus does clean. They don't overlap — they cover different parts of the empire.
What we own
Pay once, ours forever. License explicitly allows custom client work.
Ideogram Plus
$10/month — $120/year
What it is: An AI image generator that is specifically good at producing images with readable text. Most AI image tools mangle text on logos, banners, and graphics — Ideogram doesn't. It's the specialist for brand assets.
What we use it for: Logos, hero banners, merchandise mockups, social-media graphics — anywhere we need text rendered cleanly inside an AI-generated image. We already get general image generation free elsewhere; this fixes the one weakness in that workflow.
License
1,000 image generations per month, full commercial use.
02 · Why This Mix

The two big purchases (Aceternity + Tailwind Plus) cover different aesthetic territories:

  • Aceternity — cinematic / dark / luxury / futuristic. The "wow" sites.
  • Tailwind Plus — clean / professional / enterprise / e-commerce. The "trustworthy" sites.

Together they let us build any site without picking a style we don't have the tools for. One library is not enough; both libraries are not redundant.

Skipped on purpose: the $20–30/month AI website builders (v0, Bolt, Lovable, Framer). They're recurring rentals that buy speed but cap the quality ceiling. We want lifetime ownership at our scale.
