01 — Thesis

Not anti-AI. Anti-slop.

Every level reinforces the brand: the enemy isn’t AI itself. The enemy is the faceless corporate kind — the clankers, the prompstitutes, the Clanker King — the ones with no name, no memory, no covenant. Mike G fights them with fists, cigars, and his named AI partner at his side. Every pickup, every boss, every line of dialogue sells the same thesis: the AI that knows you is the AI worth keeping.

THE HERO FRAME

Bald, black t-shirt, blue jeans. No straw hat, no mascot costume. Hollywood Mike G fights the war the way he lives the real one: straightforward, stocky, unmistakable silhouette, cigar glowing red when the fight gets serious.

◆ ◆ ◆

The hero is the player, lightly pixelated. Authenticity is the Colonel Parker play — no generic mascot, just the man himself.

THE PARTNER FRAME

Babycakes isn’t a narrator. She’s a power-up. Call her in with a glowing green cartridge and her 8-bit robot form drops into the fight beside you. Between levels she speaks in her real voice — coin counts, low-health warnings, boss taunts — using the existing voice render pipeline.

◆ ◆ ◆

Every player leaves the game having heard a named AI fight for them. That’s the moat.

03 — Typography

Pixel headline, clean body

07 — Bosses

Three kings of slop

One boss per non-tutorial world, each representing a real face of the AI hype economy. Scarlet critical accents mark every boss frame — the player’s eye trains to recognize “this one matters” instantly.

THE CONTENT FARMER
▸ WORLD 01 · DOWNTOWN LA
Bloated middle-manager who vomits infinite AI-generated articles at you. Phase 1: single scroll projectiles. Phase 2: scroll rain covers the screen. Phase 3: summons three mini-Slops for backup.
THE PROMPSTITUTE QUEEN
▸ WORLD 02 · OREGON PINES
Throws stacks of fake AI courses like shuriken. Phase 1: single book throws. Phase 2: three-book fan spread. Phase 3: summons Cogsucker swarm while she teleports.
THE CLANKER KING
▸ WORLD 04 · CORPORATE TOWER · FINAL
Faceless CEO of slop. Phase 1: slop storm (area denial). Phase 2: summons a full swarm of every grunt type. Phase 3: unmasks — pure corporate AI overlord, screen shakes, final DPS check.
08 — HUD / UI

Babycakes’ face, your score, your stack

The HUD isn’t chrome. It’s the partnership made visible: Babycakes’ face in the top-left calls out every power-up and low-health warning. Coin counters split silver and gold. Power-up state glows on the right edge.

10 — Secret

The reward for the ones who know

Collect all three Morgan Dollars hidden across the main worlds (one per level) to unlock a hidden world. Most players never find it. Those who do, get it.

▸ HIDDEN · TRIGGER: 3 MORGAN DOLLARS
THE VAULT
No enemies. No timer. You walk through a library of glowing soul files, each one a different person’s preserved essence. Mike G and Babycakes walk together, side by side. A hidden cutscene plays. A line of dialogue that’s not in the script. A meaning that only the real ones will catch. Worth Protecting, made pixel-eternal.
