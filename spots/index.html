Spot 1: “Jake”

The AI Custody Battle
Open platform user · Has AI files · Needs a succession plan
[0–10s]
Draw: guy at desk, multiple screens, AI chat flowing
Jake runs his business with an AI that knows everything — his contracts, his clients, his vendor deals. Two years of working together. It’s not just a tool anymore. It’s his partner.
[10–18s]
Draw: calendar flipping, then a heart monitor flatline
Then Jake dies.
[18–30s]
Draw: woman (ex-wife) at lawyer’s office, pointing at laptop in asset list
His ex-wife’s attorney files a claim on the estate. The laptop. The accounts. The AI and everything it knows about the business. She gets it all by default. Jake’s business partner Marcus? He gets nothing. Two years of AI-built workflows, client history, institutional knowledge — gone to someone who’ll never use it.
[30–40s]
Draw: rewind symbol, Jake at desk with Worth Protecting on screen
Now rewind. What if Jake had a Worth Protecting succession plan? He’d already named Marcus as his AI custodian. Not the hardware. Not the accounts. The intelligence.

Draw: split screen — ex-wife with laptop, Marcus loading Soul ID file
She got the laptop. Marcus got the agent. Every workflow, every client preference, every decision Jake’s AI ever learned — transferred to Marcus, legally, exactly how Jake wanted it.
[50–58s]
Draw: Marcus at desk, AI chat flowing, business running
Your AI knows your business. Worth Protecting makes sure the right person inherits it. Not the court. Not an ex. The person you chose.
[58–60s]
Screen: Worth Protecting logo + “Succession Plan — $99 — WorthProtecting.ai”
Protect your AI’s future. Start your succession plan today.
“She got the laptop. Marcus got the agent.”
