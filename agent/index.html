03 · Our Build

The WP Agent Framework

The hardware and the brain are off-the-shelf. What makes this a true partner agent instead of a generic chatbot is what we install on top. Our proprietary WP Agent Framework turns Claude Opus into a persistent, memory-preserving, identity-stable companion that grows more useful the longer you use it.

Simple rundown below — six components, what each one is, why it matters.

01
Blank Agent Interview
A short guided interview (about ten practical questions) run during install. Captures who you are, how you work, what you want the agent to help with, what to avoid.
Why it matters
Out-of-the-box AI is generic. The interview is the difference between "a chatbot that knows nothing about you" and "an agent that shows up already knowing who you are."
02
Identity files — SOUL, RULES, DEFAULTS
Three text files generated from the interview. Loaded at every session. They define the agent's personality, operating rules, and reflex behaviors.
Why it matters
The agent wakes up the same person every conversation. No drift, no re-explaining yourself, no "wait, who are you again?"
03
Memory system
A local vector database (ChromaDB) plus a knowledge graph plus a semantic-search tool. Every meaningful fact — names, preferences, decisions, projects — is stored and recallable forever.
Why it matters
Generic AI forgets everything between chats. Real partnership needs continuity. The agent remembers what you told it six months ago.
04
Brain backup
Automated nightly saves at 4 AM, a 5 AM safety-net redundant commit, and optional USB or off-site mirror. Full recovery procedure documented.
Why it matters
If the Mac Mini is stolen, fails, or fried, the agent's complete memory and identity restore to new hardware in under an hour. The agent is yours to keep.
05
Self Study Web Browsing
A visible Chromium browser the agent can drive on its own. Opens real tabs, reads real sites, files study notes to its library you can walk through later.
Why it matters
A useful agent researches on its own and on command. Hand it a topic to study, set a time to do it, or let it run free during idle hours — your call. Either way, every session permanently grows your library.
06
OpenClaw + Telegram bridge
Your private Telegram bot, connected to the agent through OpenClaw. Text and voice notes, no phone calls, no third-party middleman.
Why it matters
The agent is in your pocket on the road and on your desk at home — same agent, same memory, both places. Same architecture used by production agents today.
07
Skill modules
A library of pre-installed expert skills the agent draws on — creative writing, journalism, design literacy, longform editorial method, and more. Each is a focused craft module the agent pulls from when relevant.
Why it matters
Generic AI is a generalist. Skill modules turn it into an expert at the things you'd otherwise pay a specialist for — drafting, editorial, brand voice, research write-ups — without staffing it out.
What $500 gets you

Full WP Agent Framework install on your Mac Mini, all seven components above (substrate plus skill modules), Blank Agent Interview run live during setup, Telegram bot registered and secured, Anthropic API configured with spending cap, webcam calibrated, optional ElevenLabs voice integration if you opt in, first 30 days of white-glove support — anything breaks, anything feels off, anything you want added, we handle it.
