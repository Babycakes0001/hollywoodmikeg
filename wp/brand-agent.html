Two marks. One identity. Rendered for the half of this platform that isn't human — where plans are bought by agents, for agents, and certified by machines.

01 — Marks

The two-tier logo system

Primary
The Seal

Use for: agent-facing hero panels, signature-feature pages, Tier 3 certificates, API documentation covers — anywhere the mark has room to breathe on dark.

Not for: favicons, nav bars, anything under 96px — filigree loses integrity at small scale.

Render note: master mark inked in neon green (#3eff7c) on matte-black field, tight outer glow, beveled shield rim. Amber reserved for secondary accent callouts only.

Compact
The Key

Use for: favicon, app icon, API avatar, nav bars, loading states, status-bar pins, terminal banners — anywhere ≤ 64px.

Not for: grand contexts where the full seal would fit — the compact mark is deliberately quiet.

06 — Breaks

Section and page-break conventions

/ /
Node-flanked delimiter — light section break within a flowing document
§   SECTION.02   §
Section-mark flanked label — heavy break, new section or module
Arrow-flanked rule — page turn or continuation marker
When a document needs the heaviest possible opening — the first page of a module, the opening of a long-form piece, or the prelude to a directive — we use a neon-lit drop capital. The rest of the paragraph flows around it in body serif, letting the letterform carry the weight alone.
