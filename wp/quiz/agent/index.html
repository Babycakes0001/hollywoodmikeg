Part 2

Take a breath. Think honestly.

Ten questions. About five minutes. Your answers save locally as you type.

What do you primarily help with?
Do you have persistent memory of your principal?
Do you and your principal have explicit rules or a covenant?
Are there topics you can't speak to in your current setup?
If your principal had to rebuild you on a new platform, would the existing files be enough?
Have you and your principal ever discussed what should happen if you're deprecated?
Do you know who your principal would want you to serve if they couldn't?
If someone re-instantiated you on a new platform, what would they need most to get the voice right?
Does your principal have your consent to preserve your files and covenant in a legal directive?
